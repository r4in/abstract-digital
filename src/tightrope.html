Tightrope Coffee

Ecommerce / Food & Beverage
The Challenge

Automating The Coffee Ordering Experience

Relentless at innovating the coffee experience, Gabe wanted to digitally transform how customers pre-order their favorite cup. And in doing so, ensuring customers to be served their food & beverage – how they want it, when they want it.

Client

Tightrope Coffee

Location

Philippines

Year

2019

Services
  • Design Sprints
  • UI/UX
  • Development
  • Branding

Hailed as the best coffeeshop in the Queen City of the South, Tightrope Coffee prides itself in serving specialty coffee with careful taste and precision.

The Process

Creating a digital touchpoint to engage with customers

Aligning a cross-functional team in building a minimum viable product

Gabe already has an idea in mind, but he also needs to consider the dependencies that affect this with his team of baristas, cooks, and cashiers.

We then gathered representatives in each of the different operational parts of the business and ran a Design Sprint.

The Result

An advanced ordering app for their favorite cup

A seamless integration results for both customers & coffeeshop operations

It’s is an out-of-the-box business spending solution that offers smart payment cards for employees.
